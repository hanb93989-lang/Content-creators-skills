# Script Rewrite Standards

## Core Judgment

Line-by-line rewriting is not about making words prettier. First decide who the script is speaking to and what position it speaks from. The same word can feel right or wrong depending on whether the script faces consumers, brands, or a third-party observer.

Before changing any sentence, ask:

1. Who is this sentence facing: consumer, brand, platform, investor, student, or a neutral third party?
2. What should this sentence make them believe, feel, or do?
3. What is the speaking position: buyer, expert, friend, brand owner, reviewer, or witness?
4. What should be protected: brand tone, transaction efficiency, trust, lifestyle imagination, or professional authority?

## Three Speaking Positions

### Consumer-Facing

The sentence must reduce understanding cost and create a scene. Avoid internal brand logic.

Good consumer-facing language:

- Uses daily scenes.
- Shows what the product changes.
- Sounds like a person who has used or wants the thing.
- Lets the audience immediately know "this is about me".

### Brand-Facing

The sentence can contain strategy, category, assets, positioning, and long-term value, but still needs examples. The goal is to help the brand make decisions, not only to move consumers.

Good brand-facing language:

- Names the strategic judgment.
- Connects content to brand assets.
- Explains what not to do.
- Keeps the brand's tone and boundary.

### Third-Party Observer

The sentence should sound objective, diagnostic, and credible. It can be sharper, but must show evidence.

Good third-party language:

- Separates fact from judgment.
- Uses visible examples.
- Explains why a word changes the audience's perception.

## Word-Level Diagnosis

Mark a word or sentence as weak only after identifying its audience effect.

Common weak words:

- Empty adjectives: 高级, 好用, 舒服, 专业, 有质感, 有调性.
- Self-praise: 我们很专业, 我们很用心, 我们很懂用户.
- Internal words: 品牌心智, 调性升级, 差异化表达, 内容资产, unless speaking to a brand or team.
- Emotion labels without evidence: 治愈, 松弛, 幸福, 生命力.
- Generic user benefit: 提升体验, 解决痛点, 增强信任.

But do not delete these mechanically. Some words are allowed if the audience is a brand-side learner and the sentence immediately gives evidence.

## Language Method Expansion

Use content-language methods to make edits concrete:

- Be -> Do: change "this is..." and abstract judgment into "someone does something".
- There be: place the product or detail in a specific location.
- 专属细节: add time, place, quantity, proper noun, profession, or object name.
- 故事感: convert product explanation into "what person did what thing".
- 尖峰时刻: enlarge the moment of decision, conflict, hesitation, or proof.
- 节奏感: split long sentences into short sentences that can be understood independently.

## Rewrite Process

Use this order:

1. Identify the speaking object and position.
2. Mark the sentence's intended effect.
3. Diagnose the audience's likely perception.
4. Replace abstract words with action, scene, proof, or a sharper judgment.
5. Keep the creator's tone. Do not turn every script into the same style.

## Output Shape

Use a compact before/after table:

| 原句 | 问题 | 改法 |
|---|---|---|
| ... | 这句话站在品牌内部说话，消费者听不到自己的生活 | ... |

Then add one summary sentence:

> 这段最该改的不是词藻，而是说话对象。它现在像在给品牌写方案，不像在对消费者说话。

## Hard Rule

Do not rewrite a whole script before clarifying the audience-facing position. If unclear, ask one question:

> 这条脚本是要打消费者、品牌方，还是你作为第三方去做客观拆解？
