# Content Thinking

## Master Diagnosis Order

Use this order for most content problems:

1. Purpose
2. Audience segment
3. Product
4. Brand
5. Content scene
6. Visualization
7. Transaction reason

## Purpose First

For a personal account, ask what the account wants to become:

- Is it for monetization?
- Is it for building a personal life expression?
- Is it for trust, influence, service leads, or sales?

For a brand or content operation task, start from audience, then product, then brand.

## People, Product, Brand

Audience is the bottom layer. Product is the middle medium. Brand is the light cast between audience and product.

Some brands bring natural topic traffic because of prior marketing. If a brand has strong cultural recognition, the brand word itself can become a traffic asset, such as Marshall or Leica.

## Consumer Behavior

Content must be based on consumer behavior insight.

There are at least two consumer types:

- Content consumers: respond through views, likes, comments, saves, follows, shares.
- Product consumers: respond through clicks, inquiries, orders, repurchases, word of mouth.

Good content often connects these two consumer behaviors instead of treating them separately.

## Three-Layer Value Theory

Use this as a core thinking model:

1. Functional value
   - In a product: what the product can do.
   - In an account: what useful thing the account gives, such as skills, guides, methods, or references.
2. Emotional value
   - Cute, healing, anxiety relief, identity feeling, lifestyle imagination, emotional刺点.
3. Self-realization
   - A higher layer related to self-awareness, aesthetic will, craft, public good, personal preference, life philosophy, and inner order.

Do not flatten self-realization into ordinary emotional value. For example, liking plants because they create包裹感, 治愈感, and a feeling that a home is alive belongs closer to self-realization than simple decoration preference.

## User Segmentation

The core of content is understanding people. The finer you can segment people, the more precise your content can become.

Segment by:

- Their current problem.
- Their content-consumption motivation.
- Their purchase motivation.
- Their life stage.
- Their taste and lifestyle preference.
- Their trust threshold.
- Their self-image or desired identity.

## Skill Layer

Content scene, visualization, and transaction reason are closer to technique than philosophy. They can often be solved with templates and repeatable methods after the thinking layer is clear.
