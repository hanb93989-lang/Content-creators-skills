# Account Operations

## Use Case

Use this for account covers, titles, hashtags, cold-start tagging, topic packaging, and system recognition.

## Cover Purpose Differs By Account Type

### E-Commerce Account

The cover is mainly for system recognition. The goal is to help the system classify the content and match it to purchase-oriented users. E-commerce short-video viewers should ideally click into the live room or product cart, not necessarily the homepage.

### Creator / Influencer Account

The cover is for both system recognition and recommendation-flow users. Viewers may enter the homepage and watch more videos. Covers should build trust and support follower growth.

## Cover Standard

Good covers need:

- Clear, unified picture.
- Product selling point or people signal visible.
- Unified font and color.
- A title that helps system recognition and user desire.

## Title Standard

A strong title should do three things:

1. Help the system identify content category.
2. Use keywords with existing heat.
3. Trigger desire in users who see the video.

In 0-1 cold start, prioritize the first: accurate content recognition.

## Keyword Specificity

Vertical-category proper nouns help system recognition. Broad generic words can hurt precise classification.

Examples:

- "短视频" is broad; the system may not know whether it is about operations, shooting, editing, or platform traffic.
- "DOU+", "达人相似", "定向投放" are more specific; the system can identify short-video operation content more accurately.

## Title Validation

Before publishing, copy the title without hashtags into Douyin search.

Useful title opportunity:

- Low content score: few existing contents, low competition.
- High spread score: large audience and strong spreadability.
- High search score: high search volume, strong user mind, existing search habit.

## Hashtag Structure

Generally use four types:

- Domain topic: big-category recognition.
- Content topic: detailed industry recognition.
- Industry-related hot topic: traffic borrowing.
- Self-built topic: collect real playback user-tag data and prepare an audience pool for future matrix accounts.

Cold-start priority:

`content topic > domain topic > self-built topic > hot topic`

Stable-stage priority:

`domain topic > industry hot topic > self-built topic > content topic`

## Visual Recognition

The system may sample frames to identify visual keywords. Before publishing, use the platform's recognized image keywords or recommended topic prompts to judge whether the video's picture matches the intended category.
