# Promotion Materials

## Use Case

Use this for 千川促销素材, paid traffic, direct promotion, information-flow ads, and sales-heavy e-commerce content.

## Core Rule For Direct Promotion

In direct promotion, choose only one primary message:

- Quantity is high
- Price is low

Do not force both at the same time. The user must get the promotional message as quickly as possible.

Warning: direct promotion material often works better in paid traffic than on the account homepage. It may not create stable natural traffic or live-room entrance traffic.

## Quantity-Heavy Direction

Make quantity visually shocking.

Methods:

- Change unit display: from one by one to row by row, box by box, pile by pile.
- Add an attention point without breaking category logic.
- Create the feeling of "endless quantity".
- Use movement such as pushing rows into a basket or linking many units into one long strip.

Category notes:

- Beauty: quantity plus herd effect language, such as "火爆全网" or "够用XX年".
- Food: quantity plus taste stimulation, sound, or "多种口味任选".

## Price-Low Direction

Make the price feel lower by changing the calculation method.

Methods:

- "This box is what you bought; this box is free."
- Use a calculator or visual calculation.
- "58 for one box; two boxes? Still 58."
- Show boxes/piles to make low price visible.

Category notes:

- Beauty users may prefer explicit price-calculation formats.
- Food users may perceive low price through stacked product visuals.

## Big-Text Promotion Template

Use big-text material when the core expression needs immediate recognition.

Core expression should usually contain:

1. Trust or brand endorsement.
2. Quantity, price, or applicability.
3. Function, taste, efficacy, or use scene.
4. Visual proof, such as origin, official source, product stack, or sound.

Examples of mechanisms:

- Trust: "official", "national brand", "not a no-name product".
- Applicability: one product can cover multiple uses.
- Quantity: many units or bundle value.
- Taste/function: use sound, close-up, or usage action as proof.

## Template Migration

When migrating templates across categories:

- Avoid stiff product arrangement that leaves everyday life.
- Food and beverage need more fun or taste stimulation.
- Personal care needs clearer efficacy points.
- Familiar scenes can make反逻辑 and conflict templates easier to accept.
- Increase information density with subtitles, text effects, and clear visual communication.
- Use stronger contrast through image, wording, or editing effect.

## Diagnosis Questions

- Is this material for paid traffic or natural account content?
- Is the main message quantity or price?
- Can the user understand the benefit in one second?
- Is the promotion proof visual, verbal, or both?
- Does the category require taste, efficacy, herd effect, trust, or price calculation?
