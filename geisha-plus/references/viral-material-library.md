# Viral Material Library

## Use Case

Use this reference for multi-category short-video material拆解, paid traffic素材, Douyin e-commerce挂车/挂店短视频, product seeding, and student training around爆款素材.

The source logic: do not stay inside one project category. Train category migration ability by studying how different categories turn product value into opening, scene, high-light wording, high-light shot, and visualized selling point.

## Why High-Quality Materials Matter

High-quality material tends to run better than ordinary material. For Douyin e-commerce, 巨量千川, and 巨量营销, quality material can also receive platform traffic or review incentives. For closed-loop e-commerce, natural traffic support and paid traffic support can stack.

Use this as a business reason when explaining why students should study素材 quality rather than only "creative inspiration".

## Platform Quality Baseline

Before judging creativity, check baseline quality:

- Sound: clear, fluent, no repeated noise, popping, stutter, unstable volume, or too-low/too-high volume.
- Readability: spoken content can be understood. If dialect or hard-to-understand expression is used, add Mandarin subtitles.
- Visual clarity: picture should be clear and fluent; avoid blurry low-resolution footage.
- Information value: the video should make product use, product scenario, or user decision richer.
- Interaction potential: 3-second completion, 5-second completion, full completion, watch time, CTR, likes, comments, shares.

## Material Deconstruction Sheet

For every case, extract:

1. Category
2. Content type: product display, single-person口播,真人展示, expert trust, scene demo, comparison, story, conflict, etc.
3. Video attachment or source note
4. Topic
5. Shooting formula
6. 高光话术
7. 高光镜头
8. 可视化卖点
9. Shooting scene
10. Model or persona
11. Transaction reason

Use this structure when analyzing student examples.

## Core Judgment: Scene Richness

商品卖点 must become 场景化介绍.

Judge:

- Is the product scenario rich and concrete?
- Does it include season, occasion, skin type, preference, usage habit, home environment, social relation, or purchase anxiety?
- Do person, product, and scene match?
- Does the scene create realism or immersion?

Low-quality performance:

- Only showing product appearance.
- Listing functions without use context.
- Person, environment, and product feel mismatched.

## Common Opening Engines

- Pain opening: "浴室的拖鞋这样放地上..."
- Negative opening: "不要再这样..."
- Reverse recommendation: "不建议大家买这个..."
- Curiosity/反差 opening: "谁能把鞋子穿臭了..."
- Expert trust opening: "专家研究了..."
- Question opening: "送礼怎么显贵?"
- Conflict/argument opening:争吵互动 or two-person disagreement.
- Visual surprise: dense quantity, strong comparison, unusual use, texture close-up.

## Category Migration Notes

### Apparel and Accessories

Use body effect, comfort, matching, season, and social occasion.

Visualize:

- Knee protection in air-conditioned rooms.
- Face-size effect through sunglasses.
- High waist, straight-leg, and body-shaping effects through before/after body lines.
- Comfort through long wear, movement, and close-up pressure marks.

### Beauty and Personal Care

Use wrong-way correction, fast result, before/after, and close-up operation.

Visualize:

- Wrong vs correct makeup.
- Hair volume anxiety and actual improvement.
- Skin, texture, or usage result through close-up proof.

### Jewelry, Watches, and Taste Goods

Use texture, light, sound, ritual, and owner identity.

Visualize:

- Strong side light to show texture.
- Slow rotation to make pattern flow.
- Tea table, study, incense, book, or other lifestyle scene.
- Sound of beads or material as proof.

### Shoes and Functional Wear

Use smell, comfort, height, material, and service trust.

Visualize:

- Smell association for anti-odor.
- Wearing for days without smell.
- Height increase comparison.
- Material detail and brand mark.

### Home and Storage

Use mess-before/order-after, installation convenience, capacity, cleaning path, and household anxiety.

Visualize:

- Shoes on bathroom floor as dirt anxiety.
- Open/close capacity.
- No-drill installation.
- Cleaning before/after.

### Mother and Baby

Use parent anxiety, child comfort, safety, and daily scene.

Visualize:

- Sweat, odor, breathability.
- Running/jumping without slipping or falling off.
- Scratch/pull tests for durability.
- Home floor, sofa, bed, playground, or grass scene.

### Fresh Food

Use freshness, origin, texture, juice, and cut surface.

Visualize:

- Water droplets and leaves.
- Cutting open and juice flow.
- Flesh separating cleanly from the pit.
- Macro detail of fruit surface and inside.

### Education and Books

Use parent anxiety, expert trust, grade transition, and "learn easier later" outcome.

Visualize:

- Product stack and usage plan.
- Parent-child scene.
- Before/after of learning difficulty.
- Expert or experienced-parent explanation.

### Gifts and Business Social

Use social pressure, face, price-value relation, and packaging.

Visualize:

- Gift box reveal.
- Quantity and package details.
- "Looks expensive" social topic.
- Natural product exposure before direct selling.

## How To Use In Coaching

When a student brings a product or script:

1. Identify its category and nearest case family.
2. Extract current opening, high-light wording, high-light shot, and visualized selling point.
3. Say which layer is missing.
4. Borrow one formula from another category, but adapt it to the student's product.
5. Output one revised opening, one shot list, and one transaction reason.

Do not copy case wording mechanically. Transfer the mechanism.
