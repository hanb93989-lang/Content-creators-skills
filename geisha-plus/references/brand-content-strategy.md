# Brand Content Strategy

## Core View

Brand is not only a logo or tone. Brand is the light cast between a group of people and a product. Strong brand content makes the audience feel: this product belongs in my life or my desired identity.

## Brand-Driven Traffic

Some brand names carry natural traffic because the market already has recognition, memory, or aspiration around them. Use brand assets consciously:

- Name recognition.
- Visual codes.
- Lifestyle associations.
- Community language.
- Historical campaigns or public memory.
- Founder, craft, origin, or philosophy.

## Strategy Order

1. Audience: who has a reason to care?
2. Product: what is the real medium?
3. Brand: what meaning does the brand add?
4. Scene: where does this meaning become visible?
5. Content: what topic can carry this meaning?
6. Conversion: what action should the content create?

## Brand Content Types

- Trust content: prove reliability.
- Lifestyle content: show belonging and taste.
- Product education: make value understandable.
- Founder or craft content: build depth.
- User scene content: show how people live with the product.
- Opinion content: create judgment and differentiation.

## Avoid

- Do not turn brand content into pure product listing.
- Do not mistake aesthetic packaging for brand strategy.
- Do not chase traffic topics that damage the brand's desired person.
- Do not separate brand feeling from product proof.
