# Account Plan

## First Question

Ask what the account wants to become.

Do not plan content before clarifying whether the account is for:

- Monetization.
- Trust and influence.
- Brand building.
- Personal life expression.
- Product sales.
- Private-domain conversion.
- Team or course acquisition.

## Account Diagnosis

Use:

1. Person: who is speaking?
2. Audience: who is being gathered?
3. Problem: what repeated problem does the account solve?
4. Value layer: functional, emotional, or self-realization?
5. Content columns: what recurring content will be produced?
6. Proof: why should people trust this account?
7. Business path: how does attention become value?

## Content Columns

Each column should have:

- Audience segment.
- Repeated scene.
- Core value.
- Visual format.
- Conversion or relationship function.

## Account Positioning Trap

Avoid vague positioning such as:

- "做生活方式"
- "做个人IP"
- "做干货分享"
- "做品牌内容"

Translate these into:

- For whom?
- Under what situation?
- With what judgment?
- Through what evidence?
- Toward what business result?
