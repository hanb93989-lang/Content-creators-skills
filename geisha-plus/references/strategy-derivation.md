# Strategy Derivation

## Use Case

Use this when the user asks for content strategy, brand/product/user/channel strategy, or why a product's perceived value is not being communicated.

## Market Premise

In a mature traffic cycle, supply is greater than demand. Users often do not know clearly what they want. Passive recommendation can be more efficient than active search.

Therefore, product functional competition becomes content competition. The product value chain assumed by the merchant often differs from the value chain perceived by users.

Core sentence:

> A good sale, online or offline, makes the target audience more easily understand why they need this product.

## Emotional Value Is Not No Practical Value

Do not define emotional value as empty feeling. Emotional value can be a product value-chain design if it lets users perceive:

- A new use experience
- A more efficient process
- A stronger identity
- A more desirable daily ritual
- A cost increase that users can feel and accept

## Main Logic To Main Strategy

Main logic produces main strategy. Main strategy then maps to:

- Product language
- User language
- Channel language

The strongest side of the product-user-channel triangle determines the strategy derivation.

## Product Attribute Layer

Decide the importance order of picture, copy, and BGM by product education cost.

- Low education cost: picture can lead. If the audience immediately knows what it is and how to use it, use visual impact first.
- High education cost: copy or trusted explanation must lead. For example, 3C products often need review-style content because users need a trusted person to quickly give purchase conclusions.

## People Imprint Layer

Ask: what triggered my desire to buy? This is not purchase yet. It is the "I was seeded" moment.

Split into:

- Spiritual need: identity, status, vanity, taste, self-image.
  - Use scene design: props, environment, atmosphere, lighting, places this group likes to enter.
- Functional need: performance, parameter, utility, problem-solving.
  - Use shooting technique: comparison, exaggeration, proof, endorsement, tests.

## Content Shell Layer

After product and audience logic are clear, wrap the content with a hot and relevant content shell.

Shell standard:

- Hot enough to expand the funnel.
- Related enough not to mislead the algorithm or audience.
- Able to carry the product value without feeling forced.

Do not choose a shell before understanding product attribute and people imprint.
