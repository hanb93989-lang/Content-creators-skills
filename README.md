## ✨ New: geisha-plus

`geisha-plus` is a content strategy skill for short videos, live rooms, product visualization, script diagnosis, brand content, and account strategy.

It is not a generic script generator. It is designed to think with creators and teams through concrete content judgments:

- Who the content is really speaking to
- How product value becomes visible
- Why a script does or does not work
- Whether a live room can hold the right audience at first glance
- How brand content turns into scenes, words, visuals, and reasons to buy

---

## ⭐ Featured Capability

The ones I care about most.

**Audience Segmentation** - Reconstruct concrete users from comments, questions, buying concerns, and platform language.

**Product Visualization** - Turn selling points into scenes, details, comparisons, and proof.

**Script Diagnosis** - Diagnose scripts by audience, speaking position, visual action, emotional path, and transaction reason.

**Live Room Design** - Judge live rooms by audience-scene fit, visual trust, product ladder, and transaction rhythm.

**Brand Content Strategy** - Help brand meaning become visible through people, products, scenes, and content assets.

## ✨ 新项目：geisha-plus

`geisha-plus` 是一个内容策略 skill，面向短视频、直播间、商品内容可视化、脚本诊断、品牌内容和账号策略。

它不是一个普通的脚本生成器，而是用来和创作者、团队一起做具体内容判断：

- 内容到底在和谁说话
- 产品价值如何被看见
- 脚本为什么成立或不成立
- 直播间第一眼能不能留住目标用户
- 品牌内容如何变成场景、话术、画面和购买理由

---

## ⭐ 核心能力

**用户分层** - 从评论、问题、购买顾虑和平台语言中反推出具体用户。

**商品可视化** - 把卖点转化成场景、细节、对比和证据。

**脚本诊断** - 从人群、说话对象、画面行动、情绪路径和交易理由诊断脚本。

**直播间设计** - 用受众与场景匹配、视觉信任、货盘结构和转化节奏判断直播间。

**品牌内容策略** - 让品牌意义通过人群、商品、场景和内容资产被看见。
